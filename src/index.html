Documentation

a1lon9room is an interactive 3D room you can explore right in your browser — a cozy late-night creator's workspace rebuilt as a real-time WebGL experience.

This is a fan-made project, created by the community as a tribute to the crypto influencer Alon. It is unofficial and not affiliated with or endorsed by him.

What it is

Step into a hand-crafted bedroom-studio: glowing monitors, a streaming setup, a guitar in the corner, shelves full of character, and neon washing over the walls. Everything is rendered live with Three.js and animated in real time — the chair sways, the screens flicker, the neon breathes.

The character

At the center sits the room's resident — a stylized 3D avatar lounging in the gaming chair, reacting to the scene around him. He greets you when you arrive and gives the room its personality.

Explore

  • Drag to orbit the camera and look around the room.
  • Scroll to zoom in on the details.
  • Click the screen on the wall to jump to the Twitter profile.
  • Watch live market data on the desk monitor via DexScreener.

Tech

Built with Three.js and WebGL, baked lighting for the environment, real-time shading for the character, and the Web Speech API for the voice intro. Designed to run smoothly straight from the browser.
